Sarah Knisely

Senior Member of Bob Jones Academy's Communications Committee

I am a Junior at Bob Jones Academy in Greenville, SC. I am currently a member of the school orchestra and choir and enjoy working as a designer on the Student Body Communications Committee.

I enjoy serving in my church, Suber Road Baptist Church. I play violin in the orchestra, volunteer as a helper in children's church, and stock coffee supplies for Sunday morning fellowship. I also enjoy ministering to the community through my school's choir and orchestra's trips to the Greenville Resue Misson and other similar outreaches each semester.

In my spare time I enjoy baking, reading, and exploring the potential of various graphic designing programs.
